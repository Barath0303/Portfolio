KrishiMitra AI: How We Built a Crop Risk Prediction System in 48 Hours

At DEVSPACE'25, our team — DeadEnd Developers — built KrishiMitra AI in 48 hours: an end-to-end platform predicting crop risk for Indian smallholder farmers. Over 58% of agricultural households carry outstanding debt, and our goal was to change that with data.

We integrated IMD weather records, ISRO soil health cards, and Agmarknet mandi prices into a multi-model stack — Random Forest + XGBoost for yield prediction (R² = 0.83), Logistic Regression for risk scoring (AUC-ROC = 0.89), and ARIMA + LSTM for price forecasting. I led the UI design on Streamlit. We cleared Round 1.

The core challenge was feature engineering — transforming raw sensor data into agronomic signals like Rainfall Deviation Index, Growing Degree Days, and Temperature Stress Index. This bridged the gap between raw data and meaningful predictions. The experience cemented my belief: AI built right can be a lifeline, not just a product.
